About

Hi I'm Kris, a Brooklyn based Software Engineer.

I am a full-stack software engineer, with a background in the studio arts, management, and customer service. As a conceptual artist, I am used to parsing experience using visual symbols to produce a work of art. I have always thought of art as a language of symbols-- there are patterns and rules, some explicit and some merely felt, all used in combination to produce a work of art. It is here that I find a symmetry between conceptual art and code. Seeking an engineering position that harnesses my creative abilities and theoretical passions, where problem-solving, collaboration, and growth are encouraged. Currently learning AI Facial Recognition Technology and natural language processing to explore the possibilities of machine empathy.
